üéØ What is K53 All About?

The term K53 is a file reference used when the driving test was changed to incorporate the defensive driving system.

Defensive Driving Means:

  • Being fully alert and attentive while driving
  • Maintaining clear space around your vehicle
  • Avoiding collisions with other road users
  • Giving advance warning of your intentions
