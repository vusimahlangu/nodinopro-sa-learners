K53 Testing Procedures

Vehicle Controls Check

Demonstrate knowledge of all vehicle controls including lights, indicators, brakes, and horn.

Yard Test

Perform maneuvers including alley docking, parallel parking, and three-point turns in the test yard.

Road Test

Drive on public roads while demonstrating proper observation, signaling, and positioning.

Scoring System

Understand the immediate failure faults and how points are deducted during the test.

Speed Limits
